# Tone & Voice Examples

On-brand vs. off-brand writing samples for every channel. Use these as a reference when writing or reviewing copy.

---

## Marketing Copy

### Headline

❌ **Off-brand:**
> "Leverage our cutting-edge, synergistic platform to unlock next-level workflow paradigm optimization."

✅ **On-brand:**
> "Move faster. Skip the busywork."

---

### Body Copy (Landing Page)

❌ **Off-brand:**
> "Maplerun is a best-in-class solution that facilitates the seamless integration of cross-functional business processes."

✅ **On-brand:**
> "Maplerun connects your team's tools so everyone's working from the same page — without the back-and-forth."

---

## Email

### Subject Line

❌ **Off-brand:**
> "URGENT: Don't miss this incredible opportunity!!!"

✅ **On-brand:**
> "Your Q3 report is ready"

---

### Opening Line

❌ **Off-brand:**
> "Hi [Name], I hope this email finds you well! I just wanted to reach out and touch base..."

✅ **On-brand:**
> "Hi [Name], I'm following up on the proposal we sent last week."

---

### CTA

❌ **Off-brand:**
> "Please don't hesitate to reach out at your earliest convenience if you would like to potentially explore further."

✅ **On-brand:**
> "Book a 20-minute call here →"

---

## Social Media

### Instagram Caption

❌ **Off-brand:**
> "Excited to announce the launch of our game-changing new feature!!! 🎉🎉🎉 #innovation #disruption #synergy"

✅ **On-brand:**
> "New: your reports now write themselves. Seriously. Link in bio."

---

### LinkedIn Post

❌ **Off-brand:**
> "We are pleased to share some exciting news with our valued network of professionals and stakeholders."

✅ **On-brand:**
> "We just hit 10,000 customers. Here's what we learned building to this point:"

---

## Customer Support

### Responding to a Complaint

❌ **Off-brand:**
> "I apologize for any inconvenience. The issue has been escalated to the appropriate team."

✅ **On-brand:**
> "That's frustrating — I'm sorry this happened. Let me look into it right now and get back to you within the hour."

---

### Closing a Support Ticket

❌ **Off-brand:**
> "Please let us know if there is anything else we can assist you with at this time."

✅ **On-brand:**
> "All sorted — let me know if anything else comes up!"
