# Channel Specs

Image dimensions, video lengths, copy character limits, and format requirements for every channel Maplerun uses.

## Instagram

| Format | Dimensions | Max File Size | Copy Limit |
|--------|------------|---------------|------------|
| Feed Image | 1080 × 1080 px (square) or 1080 × 1350 px (portrait) | 8 MB | Caption: 2,200 chars; Hashtags: ≤ 30 |
| Reels | 1080 × 1920 px | 4 GB | Caption: 2,200 chars |
| Stories | 1080 × 1920 px | 4 GB | On-screen text: keep to top/bottom thirds |
| Story CTA | Sticker link | — | CTA text: ≤ 35 chars |

## TikTok

| Format | Dimensions | Duration | Copy Limit |
|--------|------------|----------|------------|
| Video | 1080 × 1920 px | 15 s – 10 min (optimal: 15–60 s) | Caption: 2,200 chars; Hashtags: ≤ 10 |
| Spark Ad | Same as organic | — | Headline: 100 chars |

## YouTube

| Format | Dimensions | Duration | Copy Limit |
|--------|------------|----------|------------|
| Pre-roll Ad | 1920 × 1080 px | 12 s (skippable after 5 s) | Headline: 15 chars; Description: 70 chars |
| Long-form | 1920 × 1080 px | No limit | Title: 100 chars; Description: 5,000 chars |
| Thumbnail | 1280 × 720 px | — | Keep text to ≤ 40% of image area |

## Email

| Element | Limit | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Subject line | ≤ 50 chars | Preview text: ≤ 90 chars |
| Preheader | ≤ 90 chars | Should complement, not repeat, subject |
| Hero image | 600 px wide | Max 200 KB; include alt text |
| Body copy | 50–125 words | One primary CTA only |
| CTA button text | ≤ 25 chars | Action-oriented verb: "Start Free Trial" |

## LinkedIn (Paid)

| Format | Dimensions | Copy Limit |
|--------|------------|------------|
| Single Image Ad | 1200 × 627 px | Headline: 70 chars; Intro: 150 chars |
| Video Ad | 16:9 or 1:1 | 3–30 s; Headline: 70 chars |
| Carousel | 1080 × 1080 px per card | Headline per card: 45 chars |
