# Marketing Campaigns Skill

## Overview
This skill enables the agent to assist with planning, executing, analyzing, and reporting on Maplerun's marketing campaigns across all channels.

## When to Use
- Creating campaign briefs, timelines, or content calendars
- Drafting ad copy, email sequences, or social media posts
- Analyzing campaign performance metrics
- Summarizing or reporting on marketing results
- Segmenting audiences for targeting

## Campaign Types
| Type | Channels | Goal |
|------|----------|------|
| Brand Awareness | Instagram, TikTok, YouTube | Reach & Impressions |
| Lead Generation | Email, LinkedIn, Paid Search | MQLs / Sign-ups |
| Product Launch | All channels | Conversions & Revenue |
| Retention | Email, SMS | Churn Reduction |
| Event Promotion | Social, Email | RSVPs / Attendance |

## Campaign Brief Template
When creating a campaign brief, always include:
1. **Campaign Name** — clear, descriptive
2. **Objective** — one primary KPI
3. **Target Audience** — segment, region, persona
4. **Key Message** — single core value proposition
5. **Channels** — list all channels and formats
6. **Timeline** — start date, end date, key milestones
7. **Budget** — total and per-channel breakdown
8. **Success Metrics** — at least 2 measurable KPIs

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Awareness**: Impressions, Reach, CPM
- **Engagement**: CTR, Likes, Shares, Comments, Time-on-page
- **Conversion**: CVR, CPA, ROAS, MQLs, SQLs
- **Retention**: Open rate, Click rate, Churn rate

## Audience Segments
- **Enterprise**: Companies 500+ employees, B2B focus, longer sales cycle
- **SMB**: 10–499 employees, value-driven messaging, faster decisions
- **Consumer**: Individual users, emotion-led, mobile-first

## Content Guidelines
- Headlines: ≤ 60 characters
- Email subject lines: ≤ 50 characters, avoid spam triggers
- CTA: One clear action per creative ("Start Free Trial", "Book a Demo")
- Always A/B test subject lines and headline variants before full rollout

## Reporting Cadence
- **Weekly**: Channel performance snapshot (send to Slack #marketing)
- **Monthly**: Full campaign report with insights and recommendations
- **Post-campaign**: Retrospective with wins, misses, and next steps

## Tools & Integrations
- **CRM**: HubSpot (lead tracking, email sequences)
- **Social**: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics, SEMrush
- **Ad Creative**: Fal (AI image generation)
- **Docs**: Google Docs, Google Slides for decks
